Hi! Welcome to my Burger Foodtruck Simulator. I've made this project as part of my journey on Ironhack's SÃ£o Paulo/ Brazilpart-time web development course. You can find information about the project on its Github Page here.

Now let's get to business. Once you click on the "Start Game" button, you will start the clock and you will receive a couple of orders. To fulfill an order, you need to press the keyboard for the ingredients. The ingredients will start to show on the screen, once you feel that you are correct, press "Enter" to deliver the order.

  • Press "k" to place a slice of bacon
  • Press "s" to place salad
  • Press "u" to place a our special sauce
  • Press "l" to place a leaf of lettuce
